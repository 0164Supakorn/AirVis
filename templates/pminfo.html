What is PM2.5

PM 2.5

Particulate Matter, abbreviated as PM, is a term used for particles found in the air; including dust, soot, dirt, smoke, and liquid droplets. PM2.5 particles are given the 2.5 annotation to describe the size as being 2.5 micrometers or less. Typically these particles are so small they can only be seen with an electron microscope

" Danger! "

  • Irritation of the eye, throat, and nose
  • Irregular heartbeats
  • Asthma attacks
  • Coughing, chest tightness, and shortness of breath
